# Zinc Commercial Use Policy

## 1. Free Tier (Community License)
Zinc is free to use for:
*   Individuals & Open Source projects.
*   **Companies:** Annual gross revenue under **USD $1,000,000**.
*   Usage: Internal tools, commercial products, SaaS.
*   Condition: You are NOT required to open-source your code if you fall under this tier.

## 2. Startup License
For companies with annual gross revenue between **USD $1,000,000** and **USD $5,000,000**.
*   **Price:** $2,000 / year / company.

## 3. Enterprise License
For companies with annual gross revenue **exceeding USD $5,000,000** or **Publicly Traded** companies (e.g., NASDAQ, NYSE, HKEX).
*   **Price:** $10,000 / year / company.

**Contact:** sales@zinclang.com

## Definitions & Scope
*   **Revenue Calculation:** Based on the Gross Annual Revenue of the **Previous Fiscal Year**.
*   **Scope:** License is per **Legal Entity**. Subsidiaries that are publicly traded or exceed revenue thresholds must purchase their own license.
*   **Group Revenue:** For private companies, revenue is calculated based on the **Consolidated Group** (Parent + Subsidiaries).
